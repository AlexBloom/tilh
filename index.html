Collecting Stories

Become a Hope Practitioner

If you have a story of surrender, stillness, grief, honesty, curiosity, forgiveness, resilience, and joy, we invite you become a Hope Practitioner sharing your words and wisdom on finding faith in the things unseen. Your testimony will find someone at their crucible moment where they find themselves rapidly losing hope at end of the same rope that you were once clutching.
