The Practices

Hope is not an emotion; it is an action.

Living by faith requires complete confidence that what you hope for will come to be, and conviction of what isn't necessarily clear to you in the present. This confidence and conviction are sustained by a series of practices which will come to life through shared words and wisdom from a community of dynamic Hope Practitioners.

Become a Hope Practitioner

If you have a story of surrender, stillness, grief, honesty, curiosity, forgiveness, resilience, and joy, we invite you to become a Hope Practitioner. Sharing your words and wisdom on finding faith in the things unseen. Your testimony will find someone at their crucible moment where they find themselves rapidly losing hope at end of the same rope that you were once clutching.
