Collecting Stories

If you have a story of surrender, stillness, grief, honesty, curiosity, forgiveness, resilience, and joy, I invite you become a Hope Practitioner and share your words and wisdom on finding faith in the things unseen. Your testimony will find someone at their crucible moment where they find themselves rapidly losing hope at end of the same rope that you were once clutching.

The role of a Hope Practitioner bears great responsibility. The gift of your story will have the opportunity to inspire positive change in the life of another. This is Living Hope.
